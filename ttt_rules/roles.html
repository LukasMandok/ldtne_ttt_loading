TTT Custom Roles

Innocent:

Beacon

The Beacon gains power as teammates die, improving stats like speed and damage. If they kill an innocent, they revert to a regular innocent and take damage. If they accumulate enough power, their role is revealed, becoming a trusted figure for the team.

Clairvoyant

The Clairvoyant can see the special roles of all non-standard players. They can also kill the Jester without triggering its win condition, and after doing so, the Jester respawns as their Sidekick.

Cupid

Cupid starts with an invisible crossbow that makes two players fall in love. If used after someone's death, the effect is voided.

Lovers

Lovers split damage between them, but if one dies, the other dies of a broken heart. If on opposite teams, they form their own team to win when everyone else is dead.

Lycanthrope

The Lycanthrope gains a significant power boost if they're the last innocent standing, including increased speed, health, and damage.

Occultist

When the Occultist falls below 25% health, they are resurrected after death. They are fireproof but must beware of others being near their corpse, which will notify them of their revival.

Pharaoh

The Pharaoh places an Ankh that converts a random Traitor into a Graverobber. If the Pharaoh dies, they can be revived by the Ankh unless stolen by the Graverobber.

Graverobber

The Graverobber works against the Pharaoh and can steal their Ankh, causing their own revival instead.

Shinigami

The Shinigami respawns as a fast-moving figure with a list of traitors. They gradually lose health over time and cannot communicate.

Spectre

When killed, the Spectre haunts their killer. If their attacker dies, the Spectre is revived.

Spy

A Spy has a shop and can blend with the Traitors, but they don't know who the Traitors are, while the Traitors don't know the Spy's role.

Trapper

The Trapper detects traps and sees visual indicators when traps are used. Other trap-users can also see when the Trapper uses a trap.

Wrath

If the Wrath is killed by an innocent, they become a Traitor, but if killed by anyone else, they die normally.

Traitor:

Accomplice

The Accomplice works alongside the Traitor but doesn't know who they are. They can only buy one Traitor item and can't win alone.

Executioner

The Executioner has a target and deals extra damage to them. Killing non-targets results in a delay before a new target is assigned.

Glutton

The Glutton uses hunger to power their weapon, Devour, and can heal by eating corpses. If their hunger reaches zero, they transform into the Ravenous.

Hitman

The Hitman needs to kill their assigned target to earn credits. If the target dies another way, no reward is given.

Vampire

The Vampire must kill a player every minute to avoid Bloodlust, which damages them. While in Bloodlust, they heal for a portion of the damage they deal.

Neutral Killers:

Infested

The Infested infects others by killing them, turning them into allies. If they die, they can respawn as long as their host stays alive.

Necromancer

The Necromancer uses a defibrillator to revive dead bodies as zombies that fight for them. Zombies are slow and have limited ammo.

Serial Killer

The Serial Killer works alone, using a tracker item to see players' actions. They can steal credits but are not rewarded for kills.

Ravenous

The Ravenous must kill everyone else to win, losing all weapons except for their Devour and emitting a blood-red smoke trail.

Restless

The Restless can die multiple times, reviving each time with weakened stats until they run out of lives.
